About SeaLux Property

We connect foreign buyers with exceptional developments across South East Asia — and introduce them to the right people to make it happen.

Who we are

A curated property platform for South East Asia

SeaLux Property is a property showcase platform focused on South East Asia. We work with a small selection of carefully chosen developments, and connect interested buyers directly with the specialist sales agents for each project.

We don't sell property ourselves. What we do is make it easier for foreign buyers to discover the right development, understand the market, and speak to the right person — without the noise and pressure of a traditional agency.

Every development on the site has been selected based on location, developer track record, build quality and investment fundamentals. We only list projects we'd be comfortable recommending to a friend.

How it works

From browsing to buying

Four simple steps from discovering a development to speaking with the right agent.

01

Browse or search

Explore developments by lifestyle, requirements or budget. Every page is designed to help you understand whether a development fits your vision before you enquire.

02

Find your development

Each development page covers the location, unit types, amenities, pricing and what kind of buyer it suits best.

03

Enquire directly

Every development has a dedicated agent. Your enquiry goes straight to them — no call centres, no generic responses.

04

Get the full picture

Your agent will walk you through floor plans, payment structures, legal ownership and next steps at your pace.
