Bluroc Hua Hin

A blend of beach lifestyle and everyday convenience in the heart of Hua Hin City.

The development

Coastal living, affordably done

Developed by L9 Development Co., Ltd., Bluroc Hua Hin brings together 448 thoughtfully designed studio, one-, and two-bedroom residences across 4 floors, finished in a distinctive blend of Moroccan-inspired architecture and Mediterranean colour palettes.

Just five minutes from Hua Hin Airport and a short drive from Klai Kangwon Palace, Small Beach and Queen's Park, Bluroc puts residents close to the city's beaches, culture and dining while keeping ownership costs approachable — with completed, move-in ready units currently available.

Everything you need, close to home

Bluroc Hua Hin residents enjoy two large outdoor swimming pools, a fully-equipped fitness centre, landscaped tropical gardens, a shaded terrace, family-friendly common areas, complimentary Wi-Fi and secure parking under the building.

Whether you're here full-time or as a holiday base, the amenities are designed to keep everyday life simple and relaxed.

Pool & Wellness

Two Swimming Pools

Two large outdoor pools surrounded by lush greenery give residents space to cool off and unwind without the crowds.

Fitness Centre

A well-equipped gym is available on-site for residents who want to keep up their routine without leaving home.

Made for families

Bluroc's family rooms and communal spaces are designed to make everyone feel at home, with warm, welcoming interiors throughout the property.

Secure parking, on-site Wi-Fi and easy access to nearby schools and amenities make day-to-day family life straightforward.

Garden & Terrace

Tropical Garden

Landscaped greenery throughout the grounds creates a calm, resort-like setting right outside your door.

Terrace

A shaded terrace offers a relaxed spot to unwind or catch up with neighbours.

Beach

Bluroc is only 10mins walk to the beach and royal park. Baan Suksamakkee Beach is only 2.4 km away.
