Begin your next chapter in a connected community at the heart of Metro Manila.

The development

For creators and connectors at the heart of Metro Manila

With 1,283 thoughtfully designed studio, one-, two-, and three-bedroom residences, Laya is both a home and a community created for modern professionals, creatives and growing families.

Offering over 2,934 square metres of world-class amenities and event spaces, Laya is more than simply a residence. It is a place to connect, entertain, build and grow. Designed by internationally renowned architects and interior designers, Laya represents Shang Properties' commitment to exceptional design, craftsmanship and quality.
