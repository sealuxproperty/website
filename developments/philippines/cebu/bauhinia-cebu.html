Shang Bauhinia Residences

Ultra luxury residences overlooking Cebu Country Club, moments from Cebu IT Park.

The development

Luxury living in the heart of Cebu

Nestled within the exclusive tree-lined neighborhood of One Bauhinia Drive, Shang Bauhinia Residences offers a rare balance of tranquility and connectivity. Just minutes from Cebu IT Park and Cebu Business Park, residents enjoy extraordinary panoramic views over the city and Cebu Country Club.

Comprising 982 residences, 81 Signature residences and only 2 penthouses, Shang Bauhinia combines world-class architecture with over 3,800 sqm of hotel-grade amenities spread across two levels. Designed for both end-users and investors, it delivers Shang Properties' signature craftsmanship and attention to detail.
