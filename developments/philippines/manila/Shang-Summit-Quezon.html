Shang Summit Quezon

The Height of Elegance - A New Icon in Quezon City.

The development

Truly One of A Kind

Shang Summit is the first residential offering in Quezon City by Shang Properties and the latest flagship development in its exclusive portfolio: an unprecedented symbiosis of luxury, design, and craftsmanship. The soaring iconic towers of Shang Summit rise to over 260 meters in height, redefining the city's skyline.

The East Tower offers 1,020 luxury residences in studio, one-, two-, and three-bedroom configurations, each with stunning panoramic views of the city. Welcome to Shang Summit: an intimate collaboration on the grandest of scales between a visionary developer and the world's finest architects, designers, and artisans. Experience the Height of Elegance.

Relaxation heading

The Summit Lounge

With its soaring interiors and captivating al fresco lounge spaces, The Summit Lounge embodies the essence of community at Shang Summit.

The Summit Gallery

Offering a one-of-a-kind collection of beautifully-appointed indoor and outdoor spaces, The Summit Gallery is the events and func- tions wing exclusively for the use of residents and their guests. From intimate private dining rooms and verdant lawns and patios, to the private cinema and The Sunroom—an expansive event venue that seats over 100 guests.

The Alcove

Framed by expansive floor-to-ceiling windows that invite generous natural light, The Alcove on the upper floor of the podium offers formal and informal meeting areas, flexible co-working hot desks, quiet spaces designed for work or study, and cozy reading nooks.

Children

The Yard

The Yard at Shang Summit features one of the most comprehensive indoor and outdoor play areas in any residential development in Manila, offering everything from pool tables, video games, and board games in the Family Game Room, to exciting new playground facilities and plenty of space for the whole family to bond.

Alpine Adventures

An expansive outdoor play area crafted for kids aged 6 to 12, inspired by the graceful antelope and the clever fox.This alpine forest themed zone features bigger slides, climbing frames, a zipline, and more, all within a safe and exciting environment at your doorstep, and designed to encourage exploration, build confidence, and foster social interaction.

Leisure & Events

The Summit Ballroom

The Summit Ballroom offers a captivating venue for larger celebrations. With its stunning chandeliers and soaring ceilings, this elegant space accommodates over 100 guests, creating a truly memorable experience. Extend the joy by hosting pre- and post-event gatherings on the covered terrace, where the seamless blend of indoor sophistication and outdoor charm creates an unforgettable backdrop for any event.
