Haraya Residences

A vertical gated village by Shang Properties in Bridgetowne Estate.

The development

Luxury living at Bridgetowne Estate

Haraya Residences is Shang Properties' newest luxury development within Bridgetowne Estate, Pasig City. Designed as a vertical gated village, the project combines elegant architecture, expansive loggias and resort-inspired living spaces.

Residents enjoy immediate access to Ortigas CBD, Eastwood and Bonifacio Global City while benefiting from more than 4,700 sqm of indoor and outdoor amenities carefully curated to support wellness, recreation and community.
