The development

A mixed-use residence built for those who push hard and recover deeper

Aceller Hotel & Residence is an 8-storey mixed-use development in Soi Ta-iad, Phuket's well-known fitness and long-stay lifestyle district. The residential collection offers 371 studio and 303 one-bedroom condominiums across Buildings B, C and D, designed for disciplined daily living — from training and recovery to remote work.

With a sloped jogging track (the first and only of its kind in Soi Ta-iad), a half-Olympic pool, landscaped recovery gardens and a standalone clubhouse, Aceller is designed as much for everyday performance as everyday living.
