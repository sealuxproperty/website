Residence Hotel Investment
The investment

A branded hotel asset in Phuket's global training destination

Aceller Hotel & Residence includes a dedicated 197-key hotel building, developed under a global hotel partnership with TRYP by Wyndham. Buyers can purchase individual hotel units as an investment asset, positioned within Soi Ta-iad — Phuket's best-known fitness, training and long-stay lifestyle district.

With strong and consistent rental demand from international visitors who stay for training, wellness and recovery routines, Aceller is designed for both everyday hospitality use and long-term investment returns.
